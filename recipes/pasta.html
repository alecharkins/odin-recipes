Gorgonzola Arugula Pasta

Description

This is a simple pasta dish that takes advantage of the pepperiness of arugula and cracked black pepper, combined with the sharp and creamy flavor of gorgonzola cheese

It should only take 30 minutes from start to finish and leave you with an elevated mac and cheese for adults.

Ingredients

  • Arugula
  • Farfalle
  • Butter
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Melt down the butter and block of gorgonzola cheese
  2. Meanwhile, boil the pasta
  3. Once the pasta is close to al dente, remove and toss in the melted sauce. Add arugula.
  4. Plate and serve with plenty of cracked black pepper/li>
