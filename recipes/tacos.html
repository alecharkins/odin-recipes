Jalapeno Chicken Tacos

Description

These jalapeno chicken tacos come together quickly and easily with a simple marinade for the chicken that is flavorful and juicy, along with a refreshing watermelon slaw to bring some sweetness and tang. We begin by blending up jalapenos, cilantro, olive oil, pepper, garlic, and a pinch of salt and sugar. We take this marinade and let our chicken rest in it for at least 30 minutes before roasting in the oven for 20-25 minutes

As the chicken is roasting, we make our slaw. Simply diced watermelon with gochugaru, fish sauce, sugar, salt, pepper and a little bit of rice vinegar. Once the chicken is done, we plate our tacos with the chicken, slaw. Serve with cilantro and lime.

Ingredients

  • Jalapeno
  • Fish sauce
  • Limes
  • Chicken thighs
  • Gochugaru
  • Watermelon
  • Cilantro
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Blend the marinade ingredients together and toss the chicken in the marinade. Let sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Roast the chicken at 425 degrees for 20-25 minutes
  3. Gather the slaw ingredients and toss together in a bowl
  4. Remove the chicken from the oven. Warm a tortilla and fill with the chicken and watermelon slaw
  5. Serve with cilantro and a lime wedge
