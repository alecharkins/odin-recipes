Gochujang Caramel Cookies

Description

These cookies resemble a simple sugar cookie with the added umami and spice that comes from gochujang. The gochujang also adds a wonderful color to the cookie that looks so good you almost won't want to eat it...almost.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Baking Soda
  • Egg
  • Brown sugar
  • Granulated sugar
  • Gochujang
  • Vanilla
  • Salt
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine the gochujang, brown sugar, and 1 tbsp of butter in a small bowl and stir together. Let that sit.
  2. In a stand mixer or mixing bowl, beat together the sugar, egg, cinnamon and vanilla.
  3. Once that mixture is fluffy, add the salt, baking soda, and flour and whisk together.
  4. Let chill in the fridge for 30 minutes and then remove. Stir in the gochujang mixture without overmixing.
  5. Cook in the oven for 11-13 minutes
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool before eating!
